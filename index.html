    Some common dental problems include:
    • Cavity: Holes in the teeth that are caused by decay. They are typically treated with fillings to restore the tooth’s structure.
    • Gum disease: This is inflammation of the gums caused by plaque buildup. Treatment may involve professional dental cleaning, antibiotics, and improved oral hygiene habits.
    • Bad breath: This is often caused by poor oral hygiene, gum disease, or certain foods. Treatment involves improving oral hygiene habits, addressing underlying dental issues, and possibly using mouthwash or other oral care products.
    • Toothache: Pain or discomfort in or around a tooth, often caused by decay, infection, or injury. Treatment depends on the underlying cause and may include fillings, root canal therapy, or extraction.
